Rythu Bharosa Scheme:
Provides financial support to farmers in Andhra Pradesh.
Eligibility: Farmers with agricultural land.
Documents: Aadhaar card, land documents, bank account.
Application: Apply through village secretariat.

PM Kisan Scheme:
Provides ₹6000 per year to farmers.
Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers.
Documents: Aadhaar card, bank account, land records.
Application: Apply online or through agriculture office.

Ayushman Bharat:
Provides free health insurance up to ₹5 lakh.
Eligibility: Economically weaker families.
Documents: Aadhaar card, ration card.
Application: Register at empaneled hospitals.
 
MGNREGA:
Provides 100 days of wage employment for rural households.
Eligibility: Rural adults.
Documents: Aadhaar card and job card.
Application: Apply at panchayat office.

YSR Aarogyasri Scheme:
Provides free medical treatment for poor families in Andhra Pradesh.
Eligibility: Families below poverty line.
Documents: Aadhaar card, ration card.
Application: Apply through government hospitals.
